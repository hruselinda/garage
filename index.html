About us

Why choose us

Welcome! Based in Sutton, Surrey, we are one of the longest-serving garages in our local area. For more than 35 years, we've retained our independent status and remained at the same premises. Throughout this time, we’ve built lasting relationships with our customers and a vast amount of automotive trade experience. We are one of the few garages in the area to work on all manufacturer makes and models.

Our services extend into all nearby areas in South London. From our scheduled and interim car servicing to MOT testing, Rose Hill Park Garage delivers a friendly, personalised service at fair, transparent prices. We have known most of our customers for many years and they return to use us for their car servicing and vehicle repair requirements because of our attentive and caring approach. Our company is happy to provide advice at all times and we’re committed to keeping your vehicle in a safe, roadworthy condition.

Our garage

When you leave your vehicle in our care, it will be repaired or maintained by one of our own fully qualified technicians, mechanics or tyre fitters. Rose Hill Park Garage only hires members of staff from inside the Sutton and Surrey area with the same passionate approach to automotive care as our company. Whenever you choose us for a service or repair work, we’ll treat your vehicle as if it were our own. All repairs meet defining industry standards and we personally guarantee our workmanship.

Our garage is a VOSA-approved MOT testing centre equipped with advanced testing facilities linked to a national database. Approximately 28 days before your next MOT test is due, we send out a reminder to ensure you have a new certificate in place with plenty of time to spare. Rose Hill Park Garage offers free written quotations on a no-obligation basis and we welcome enquiries from new and existing clients in Sutton, Surrey and other South London areas.
